B.Fun Facts

  1. The Sun is 93 million miles away from Earth.
  2. Neutron stars can spin 600 times per second.
  3. There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth.
  4. A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus.
  5. Jupiter has 79 known moons.
  6. Astronauts grow taller in space.
  7. Black holes can warp time.
  8. Water ice exists on the Moon.
  9. Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system.
  10. The first living thing in space was a dog named Laika.
